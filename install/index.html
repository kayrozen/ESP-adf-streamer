ESP-ADF Streamer

Internet radio → ESP32 decode → Bluetooth A2DP speaker

Step 1 — Enter credentials

Fill in your WiFi and Bluetooth details first. They will be sent to the device automatically right after flashing.

SSID is required when a password is set.
Exactly as printed on the speaker label. Leave blank to auto-scan. Invalid format — example: A4:C1:38:01:02:03
Step 2 — Flash & Configure

Requires Chrome or Edge with the board connected via a data USB cable. Target: ESP32-WROVER (TTGO T7 Mini32 V1.5) · 16 MB flash · 8 MB PSRAM

Re-configure only

Already flashed? Update credentials without reflashing.
