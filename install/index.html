ESP-ADF Streamer

Internet radio → ESP32 decode → Bluetooth A2DP speaker

Step 1 — Flash firmware

Requires Chrome or Edge with the board connected via a data USB cable.
Target: ESP32-WROVER (TTGO T7 Mini32 V1.5) · 16 MB flash · 8 MB PSRAM

Step 2 — Configure & Provision

Fill in your WiFi and Bluetooth details, then click Provision via UART. The page opens a Web Serial connection and sends the credentials to the device. You can do this before or after flashing — the device listens on every boot.

SSID is required when a password is set.
Exactly as printed on the speaker label. Leave blank to auto-scan. Invalid format. Example: A4:C1:38:01:02:03
Enter your credentials above, then click Provision.
